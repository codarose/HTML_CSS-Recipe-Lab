Spicy Ginger Mussel Stir Fry

I tasted this stir fry for the first time when i was 7 years old. Fast foward to adulthood, instead of eating out at restaraunts, I decided to learn how to cook this dish. With a little tweaking to my own taste buds, I can now say I've perfected the recipe to my likings. Now instead of enjoying this dish at a restaraunt, I can now enjoy it at the comfort of my home.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb mussels (frozen/fresh)
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 1 in ginger
  • 2 green chilis (can also use red for more spice)
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 green onions (sliced)
  • 2 table spoon oil

Sauce:

  • 1 tsp chicken boullion
  • 1 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Add garlic cloves into frying pan
  2. Add 2 table spoon oil
  3. Stir the garlic cloves around until brown
  4. Add mussels and continue to stir .
  5. Add the sauces, chicken boullion, tbsp oyster sauce, dark soy sauce, and black pepper to the pan and continue to stir.
  6. When the mussels are near cooked, toss in the ingredients, ginger, green chilis, shallot, and green onions. Continue to stir until cooked.
